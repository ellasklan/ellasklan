Welcome to the Sklan Molecular virology lab

Positive-strand RNA viruses encompass over one-third of all virus genera and include numerous pathogens. These viruses replicate in the cytoplasm building their replication complexes on modified host intracellular membranes. Our major goal is to better understand the interactions of these pathogens with their host cells using Hepatitis C virus, dengue virus and Zika virus as our major models. Our current focus is interactions leading to viral replication complex formation. We hope that our understanding of these viruses at the basic science level will contribute at the applied level as well.
